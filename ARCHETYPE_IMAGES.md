# Archetype Image Suggestions (Hollywood + Animated)

Use these as “vibe references” (not 1:1 copies). Aim for clean, bright, pink/white-friendly lighting and bold silhouettes.

## Obsidian Introvert (Black Cat energy)
- **Hollywood vibe refs:** Jenna Ortega, Winona Ryder (90s), Rooney Mara, Timothée Chalamet (moody editorial)
- **Animated vibe refs:** Wednesday Addams (animated takes), Raven (Teen Titans), Marceline (Adventure Time), Nico Robin (One Piece)
- **Look:** dark monochrome, sharp tailoring, minimal jewelry, cool-toned shadows
- **Shot ideas:** 3/4 profile, direct eye contact, half-smirk, hands in pockets
- **Keywords:** “noir portrait”, “soft rim light”, “black cat energy”, “minimalist”, “dramatic contrast”

## Radiant Companion (Golden Retriever energy)
- **Hollywood vibe refs:** Zendaya (bright editorial), Florence Pugh (warm), Ryan Reynolds (playful), Chris Evans (friendly)
- **Animated vibe refs:** Rapunzel (Tangled), Anna (Frozen), Star Butterfly (SVTFOE), Aang (ATLA)
- **Look:** warm pastels, sunshine highlights, cozy textures, big smile
- **Shot ideas:** candid laugh, slight motion blur, outdoor golden hour, confetti
- **Keywords:** “golden hour”, “warm glow”, “joyful”, “bubbly”, “soft pastel background”

## Blueprint Architect (The Future-Proofer)
- **Hollywood vibe refs:** Emma Watson (clean classic), Dev Patel (polished), Keira Knightley (structured), Oscar Isaac (sleek)
- **Animated vibe refs:** Violet Parr (Incredibles), Tiana (Princess and the Frog), Tony Stark (animated variants), Korra (ATLA universe)
- **Look:** structured outfits, clean lines, subtle tech/blueprint motifs, calm confidence
- **Shot ideas:** desk + notes, city skyline, “planner energy”, crisp studio light
- **Keywords:** “editorial studio”, “clean composition”, “architectural lines”, “modern”, “intentional”

## Kinetic Maverick (Chaos catalyst)
- **Hollywood vibe refs:** Margot Robbie (high energy), Pedro Pascal (chaotic charm), Doja Cat (bold), Jack Black (wild fun)
- **Animated vibe refs:** Harley Quinn (animated), Jinx (Arcane), Deadpool (animated variants), Kim Possible
- **Look:** neon accents, asymmetry, dynamic poses, playful “messy” styling
- **Shot ideas:** mid-jump, wind-swept hair, street lights, roller rink, arcade
- **Keywords:** “dynamic motion”, “neon”, “punk pop”, “high energy”, “action pose”

## Sentimental Sincerist (The Micromancer)
- **Hollywood vibe refs:** Saoirse Ronan (soft), Andrew Garfield (gentle), Lily Collins (romantic), Tom Holland (sweet)
- **Animated vibe refs:** Belle (Beauty and the Beast), Hiccup & Astrid (HTTYD), Kiki (Kiki’s Delivery Service), Peeta (animated-inspired)
- **Look:** soft knits, hand-written notes, warm indoor light, cozy romance
- **Shot ideas:** holding a tiny gift, close-up hands, handwritten letter, coffee steam
- **Keywords:** “soft lighting”, “cozy”, “romantic”, “handwritten”, “small gestures”

## Couple Image Style (for Results Card)
- **Format:** 16:9, cinematic crop, strong subject separation
- **Palette:** whites + blush pinks; allow one accent color per archetype (black for Obsidian, gold for Radiant, steel/blue for Blueprint, neon for Kinetic, warm cream for Sentimental)
- **Avoid:** busy backgrounds, tiny faces, heavy text overlays
